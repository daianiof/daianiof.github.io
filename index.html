About the founder

Hi, I'm Daiani

For more than a decade, I have supported executives and teams in fast-paced global organizations, most recently within the Office of the President and CEO at Qualcomm. Before that, I served as a contracted team lead at Google and partnered with senior leaders at Baker Tilly, BASF, and KPMG. These experiences shaped how I anticipate needs, connect people and priorities, and create systems that allow leaders to focus on meaningful work. Based in San Diego, I bring a global perspective through my fluency in Portuguese, English, and Spanish.

I founded BotanIA to help business owners and entrepreneurs thrive through a new kind of support ecosystem. Inspired by the way nature works, where every element has a role in sustaining growth, I design intuitive intelligence solutions through custom AI agents that become an extension of your team. These agents help manage contracts, reports, scheduling, CRM organization, and other operational responsibilities, creating more space for leaders to focus on vision, relationships, and growth.

BotanIA represents the connection between nature and technology. The name combines botany, my personal passion and the study of how living systems grow, with IA, the way we say artificial intelligence in Portuguese, honoring my Brazilian roots. Just like a thriving ecosystem, I believe technology works best when every intelligent element supports the growth of the whole.
